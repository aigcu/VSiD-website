Our Mission

The VSID (Very Small Image Detection) system will be implemented in various locations on a college/university campus. It will consist of a camera connected to microcontrollers with onboard Wi-Fi. A TinyML model will be trained to detect thumbs up or thumbs down gestures from the video stream from students and faculty. The microcontroller will continuously run the model and monitor the video stream. The results of the model, collected over a set time period, will be sent to a remote database via Wi-Fi. Data analysis will be performed on the results, which will be displayed and used to indicate a student/faculty satisfaction rate.
